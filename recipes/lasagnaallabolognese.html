Lasagna alla Bolognese

←

Description

A communal dining experience where raw ingredient are cooked in a simmering,spicy or mild broth at the table .

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Bechamel sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Cooking Process

  1. Cook meat with tomato sauce.
  2. Prepare bechamel sauce.
  3. Layer pasta sheets, meat sauce, and bechamel.
  4. Repeat layers.
  5. Bake until golden.
