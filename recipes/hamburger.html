Hamburger

←

Description

A communal dining experience where raw ingredient are cooked in a simmering,spicy or mild broth at the table .

Ingredients

  • Ground beef patty
  • Burger buns
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Cheese
  • Ketchup and mustard

Cooking Process

  1. Season ground beef and form patties.
  2. Grill or fry patties.
  3. Toast burger buns.
  4. Place patty on bun and add toppings.
  5. Serve hot.
