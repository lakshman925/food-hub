Apple Pie

←

Description

A communal dining experience where raw ingredient are cooked in a simmering,spicy or mild broth at the table .

Ingredients

  • Apples
  • Pie crust
  • Sugar
  • Cinnamon
  • Butter

Cooking Process

  1. Prepare pie crust.
  2. Mix apples with sugar and cinnamon.
  3. Fill crust with apple mixture.
  4. Cover with top crust.
  5. Bake in oven until golden.
