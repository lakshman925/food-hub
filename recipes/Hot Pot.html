Chinese Hot Pot

←

Description

A communal dining experience where raw ingredient are cooked in a simmering,spicy or mild broth at the table .

Ingredients

Broth Base

  • Chicken or vegetable broth – 2 liters
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 5 slices ginger
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp chili oil
  • Salt to taste

Main Ingredients

  • Sliced beef or chicken
  • Shrimp or fish balls
  • Tofu cubes
  • Mushrooms
  • Napa cabbage
  • Spinach
  • Carrots
  • Corn pieces
  • Noodles

Cooking Process

  1. Pour broth into a large pot.
  2. Add garlic, ginger, soy sauce, and chili oil.
  3. Bring the broth to a boil.
  4. Place the pot in the center of the table.
  5. Add meat, vegetables, tofu, and noodles into the boiling broth.
  6. Cook ingredients for 1–3 minutes.
  7. Remove cooked food and eat with dipping sauce.
