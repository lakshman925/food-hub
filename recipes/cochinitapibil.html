Cochinita Pibil

←

Description

A communal dining experience where raw ingredient are cooked in a simmering,spicy or mild broth at the table .

Ingredients

  • Pork
  • Achiote paste
  • Orange juice
  • Garlic
  • Banana leaves

Cooking Process

  1. Marinate pork with achiote and orange juice.
  2. Wrap pork in banana leaves.
  3. Slow roast until tender.
  4. Shred the meat.
  5. Serve with tortillas.
