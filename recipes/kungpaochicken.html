Kung Pao Chicken

←

Description

A communal dining experience where raw ingredient are cooked in a simmering,spicy or mild broth at the table .

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast (diced)
  • Peanuts
  • Dried red chilies
  • Garlic
  • Ginger
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Cornstarch

Cooking Process

  1. Marinate chicken with soy sauce and cornstarch.
  2. Heat oil and fry dried chilies.
  3. Add garlic and ginger.
  4. Add chicken and stir fry.
  5. Add sauce mixture and peanuts.
  6. Cook until sauce thickens.
