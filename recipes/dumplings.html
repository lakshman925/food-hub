Chinese Dumplings

←

Description

A communal dining experience where raw ingredient are cooked in a simmering,spicy or mild broth at the table .

Ingredients

Dough

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • ½ cup warm water
  • 1 pinch salt

Filling

  • 200 g ground pork or chicken
  • 1 cup finely chopped cabbage
  • 2 green onions (chopped)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 tsp grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • ½ tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper

Dipping Sauce

  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1 tsp chili oil (optional)
  • 1 tsp sesame oil

Cooking Process

  1. Mix flour and salt in a bowl.
  2. Add warm water slowly and knead into smooth dough.
  3. Cover the dough and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  4. Mix ground meat, cabbage, green onions, garlic, ginger, soy sauce, sesame oil, salt, and pepper.
  5. Roll the dough into small circular wrappers.
  6. Add 1 tablespoon filling in the center of each wrapper.
  7. Fold and seal the dumpling edges.
  8. Boil for 5–7 minutes or steam for 10 minutes until cooked.
  9. Serve hot with dipping sauce.
